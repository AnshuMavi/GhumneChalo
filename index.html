Popular Destinations

Goa

Goa is the most popular destination for beachside holidays in India. It is the smallest state in the country by area, located on the southwestern coast. The region is blessed with beautiful scenic views, magnificent beaches, glorious natural landforms, tons of popular tourist attractions, and much more.

uttarkhand

Uttarakhand, often called "Dev Bhoomi" (Land of the Gods), is special for its stunning natural beauty, rich religious history, and diverse culture, including the Himalayas, numerous Hindu temples, and the iconic Char Dham pilgrimage sites.

Shimla

Shimla, known as the "Queen of Hills," is special for its colonial heritage, stunning natural beauty, and role as the former summer capital of British India, offering a unique blend of history, culture, and breathtaking scenery.

Featured Hotels

Novotel Goa Resort

Set in a Balinese-style garden, this polished resort is an 11-minute walk from Candolim Beach, and 6 km from the 17th-century Fort Aguada and lighthouse.

The Rosefinch Sarovar Portico

Situated in Bhīm Tāl, 1.4 km from Bhimtal Lake, The Rosefinch Sarovar Portico features accommodation with a shared lounge, free private parking and a restaurant. With free WiFi, this 4-star hotel offers room service and a 24-hour front desk. The property is non-smoking and is set 21 km from Naini Lake.

Hotel Willow Banks

Established in 1871, the colonial-style Hotel Willow Banks enjoys beautiful views of the Shimla Valley and mountains. About 450 metres from Christ Church and Shimla City, it has a free Wi-Fi, a sauna and hot tub.
